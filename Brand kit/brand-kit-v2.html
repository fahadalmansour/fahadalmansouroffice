03 — Construction & Specs (NEW)

How the mark is built

The logo is constructed on a 240×240 unit grid. Outer ring r=100, inner accent ring r=88. Letter heights anchor to y=66 (top) and y=174 (bottom). Following these rules at every scale keeps the mark rigorously consistent.

Construction grid
Geometry on a 12-unit grid, anchored to the central crosshair.
ViewBox 240 × 240
Outer ring r=100, stroke-width=5
Inner ring r=88, stroke-width=1.4
F stem 14×108 @ (58, 66)
A apex (162, 66) · base 130–194
Clear space
Always leave space equal to x — the height of the F top bar — around the logo on all sides. No type, image, or graphic enters this zone.
x = 14 units (in 240-unit grid)
x = ~5.8% of total mark width

09 — Usage Rules

Do & Don't

Six rules that protect the mark. Following them keeps the brand consistent across every surface.

✓ DO
Maintain clear space
Leave space equal to x (height of F top bar) around the logo on all sides.
✓ DO
Use 24px minimum
Below 24px the inner ring and crossbar lose legibility.
✓ DO
Pick the right variant
Reversed for dark, mono for single-color print, primary for everything else.
✗ DON'T
Don't recolor freely
Use only the eight palette colors. No gradients applied to the mark itself.
✗ DON'T
Don't squish or stretch
The mark is a perfect circle. Always scale uniformly.
✗ DON'T
Don't add effects
No drop shadows, bevels, or outer glows. The mark is flat by design.
